Hi, I'm Carolyn Allen

I am a data scientist plunging into the world of software development. I love writing programs that solve problems efficiently and creatively.

After graduating from college with a degree in bioinformatics, I began working at Talus Bioscience. At Talus, I designed a dashboard for automated data visualization. I built gopher, a open-source python tool to perform quantitative gene ontology enrichment for protemics. I have utilized Pytorch to leverage mass spectrometry data processing and post-processing analysis.

Interests

I am passionate about building high quality software. When I'm not at work, I enjoy long-distance running, playing classical piano, and spending time with my family.
